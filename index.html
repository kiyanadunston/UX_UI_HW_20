Up, Away

Up, Away is an all-in-one, post-pandemic, mobile app that doubles as a travel agent and communication center for solo and group travelers.

All your travel needs, all in one place.

Learn more

Objectives

I conducted 5 interviews with the goal of exploring participants’...

  • Current trip planning process
  • 'Ideal' trip planning process
  • Common challenges when trip planning

Last updated 3 mins ago

Research

Here's what some of the participants said:

"The biggest pain point for me is trying to get all the information together in a very short amount of time, because a lot of times you're trying to work out the details, by the time you come back to book everything the prices have already changed."

“I need it to kind of be fast paced. I don't want to read too much. I don't want to watch too long. So, I need to get as much information in as little amount of time as possible.”

“I dislike not having a one-stop shop, as the trip planning process is too time consuming and disjointed.”

“The ideal trip planning process would be somebody else planning it for me.”

Last updated 3 mins ago

Key Findings

Through research, I discovered that participants:

  • Travel the same, or more, post-pandemic
  • Prioritize spending minimal time when trip planning
  • Prioritize using minimal tools and platforms when trip planning
  • Prefer group travel

Last updated 3 mins ago

Problem Statement

I believe that travelers are spending valuable time and effort on trip planning, feeling stalled throughout the process, and sometimes quitting.

We might be able to help by providing a streamlined, centralized place for all trip planning components.

ROBE

The ROBE app allows you to digitize and organize your wardrobe, create and schedule outfits, keep track of and schedule laundry, request a custom storage consultation, and more. The app is designed to make users feel more organized and confident than ever before, with the app doing most of the 'dirty' work for them.

The app tagline.

Objectives

We conducted 5 1v1 user interviews to discover...

  • How people currently organize their wardrobe
  • Common challenges people face when organizing their wardrobe

  • What role wardrobes play in people's daily lives
